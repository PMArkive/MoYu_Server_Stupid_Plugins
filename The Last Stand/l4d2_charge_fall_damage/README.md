# [L4D2] Charge Fall Damaga

### Introduction
- Take fall damages while being charged.

<hr>

### Installation
1. Put **plugins/l4d2_charge_fall_damage.smx** to your _plugins_ folder.
1. Put **gamedata/l4d2_charge_fall_damage.smx** to your _gamedata_ folder.
